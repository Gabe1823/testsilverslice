About Me, The Food Man


Business Biography

With Passion For Real, Good Food

My bakery has been developing look-a-like gluten-free products since 2009. Since the pandemic,the company has been developing and wholesaling gluten-free and plant based-foods with a focus on pizzas. The company wants to satisfy the growing demand during uncertain times by wanting to increase affordable health food to low income communities locally, regionally and nationally. With the growth plan will be able to create jobs.Having a resilient food brand is the solution in order to move through uncertain times with confidence and predictability. The company wants to commit to the future,rethink risk and embrace collaboration that can help my small company fight above weight class,embrace new technologies and unlock growth.My company has been pivoting through the changing COVID 19 safety rules, mask requirements and vaccine mandates, supply chain delays, rising inflation,a labor shortage and other challenges as it clawed its way to victory.
